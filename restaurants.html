Ellen's Must-Try Restaurants

Of course, there are the iconic, you-literally-can't-say-you-went-here-unless-you've-had-them-multiple-times options.

  • Ian's Pizza
  • Tutto Pasta
  • Paul's Pelmeni
  • QQ's
  • McDonald's on Regent between 12am and 2am for the limited menu
  • Basset Street Brunch Club when your parents are in town
  • Short Stack
  • Belair Cantina
  • Parthenon

Then, there are the restaurants you will probably never hear about outside of here, but you will not want to miss.

  • Monsoon Siam for the best Thai food in Madison
  • Estacion Inka for a quick and hugely underrated Peruvian option
  • Buraka for the best Ethiopian
  • Maharani Indian Restaurant
  • DLUX
  • Coopers Tavern
  • Canteen
  • Fairchild for a fancy date when you're feeling mature and adventurous. I do recommend that you only go here if you actually like to try new things.
