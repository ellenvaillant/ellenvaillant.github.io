Ellen's Must-Try Restaurants

Of course, there are the iconic, you-literally-can't-say-you-went-here-unless-you've-had-them-multiple-times options.

  • Ian's Pizza
  • Tutto Pasta
  • Paul's Pelmeni

Then, there are the restaurants you will probably never hear about outside of here, but you will not want to miss.

  • Fairchild, for a fancy date when you're feeling mature and adventurous
  • Monsoon Siam, for a delivery type of night
  • Estacion Inka, for a nearby and hugely underrated option
  • Buraka, for the best Ethiopian
  • Maharani Indian Restaurant, because I literally want to try everything on their menu
